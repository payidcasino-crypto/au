Recognizing the Signs of Problem Gambling

Problem gambling can develop gradually. It is important to continuously assess your relationship with gaming. You may be at risk if you find yourself experiencing any of the following:

  • Chasing Losses: Trying to win back money you have lost by depositing more funds than you originally intended.
  • Hiding Your Activity: Lying to family or friends about how much time or money you spend playing online pokies Australia.
  • Borrowing Funds: Using credit cards, taking out loans, or borrowing money from loved ones specifically to fund your PayID pokies casino account.
  • Obsessive Thinking: Constantly thinking about your next gambling session, or feeling restless and irritable when you attempt to stop playing.
  • Gambling to Escape: Using casino games as a coping mechanism to avoid stress, anxiety, or depression in your daily life.

Loss Limits

Restrict the maximum amount of real money you can lose during a specific timeframe. This prevents catastrophic bankroll wipeouts during high-volatility sessions.

Session Limits
